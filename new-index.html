Who we are

A bakery built on
inclusion and community

Becca’s Cakes & Bakes began when my little sister was diagnosed with coeliac disease and I saw how quickly food became something she had to be cautious of instead of something she could simply enjoy. Watching someone I love navigate labels, cross-contamination, and constant second-guessing changed the way I see food entirely.

I started baking to change that — creating cakes and bakes that are completely gluten-free and dairy-free but made to be shared by everyone at the table.

Based in Northern Ireland, every order is made with care, attention, and a real understanding of how important “safe” food truly is.
