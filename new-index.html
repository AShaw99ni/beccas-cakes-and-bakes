Who we are

A bakery born from
necessity and love

When Becca discovered she could no longer eat gluten or dairy, she refused to settle for tasteless alternatives. After months of experimenting in her kitchen, she created something truly special — bakes that nobody could tell were free from anything at all.

Based in Northern Ireland, every bake leaves Becca's kitchen made with care, creativity, and an obsessive commitment to flavour. Because everyone deserves a proper slice.
