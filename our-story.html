How it all started

A kitchen, a diagnosis,
and a whole lot of determination

Becca's Cakes and Bakes was born out of necessity and grew into a passion. When Becca discovered she could no longer eat gluten or dairy, she faced a choice that so many people with dietary restrictions know all too well — settle for joyless alternatives, or figure it out yourself.

She chose to figure it out. After months of experimenting in her kitchen — testing flours, perfecting textures, and refusing to compromise on taste — she realised her bakes were genuinely special. Friends and family agreed. And so, Becca's Cakes and Bakes was born, right here in Northern Ireland.

Meet Becca

"I just wanted to make something people could eat without worry — and maybe enjoy a little too much."

Becca is a home baker turned small business owner with a genuine love for making people feel included. Based in Northern Ireland, she brings warmth, creativity, and an obsessive attention to detail to every bake that leaves her kitchen.

When she's not experimenting with new recipes, she's sharing her process on TikTok, connecting with the growing community of people who understand the joy of finding a cake you can actually eat. Her philosophy is simple: great baking shouldn't come with an asterisk.
